Talk: Dev life hacks using serverless

Serverless lets us build web apps, but at what cost to the developer experience? Let's look at serverless beyond a cost-efficient, super fast infrastructure and more as a tool we can't live without. In this talk, I will walk through a few of my favorite hacks for getting serverless to deliver ergonomics to developers...
